# Employee Onboarding Procedure

Version: 3.2 | Effective: March 1, 2025

## Overview

This procedure outlines the steps for onboarding new employees at Local AI Assistant. The process typically spans the first two weeks of employment.

## Pre-Arrival (3 Days Before Start Date)

1. **IT Setup**
   - Create email account and Slack workspace invitation
   - Provision laptop and necessary software licenses
   - Set up access to internal systems (GitHub, Jira, Confluence)
   - Prepare security badges and building access

2. **HR Preparation**
   - Complete employment paperwork (W-4, I-9, direct deposit)
   - Enroll in benefits packages
   - Schedule orientation session
   - Assign an onboarding buddy

## Day 1 — Welcome Day

### Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
- Welcome meeting with HR and team lead
- Office tour and introductions to key team members
- IT setup assistance and equipment handover
- Review of company handbook and policies

### Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
- Team lunch with your department
- Introduction to core tools and systems
- Review of role expectations and key performance indicators
- Set up 30-60-90 day goals with your manager

## Week 1 — Immersion

### Daily Activities
- Complete mandatory compliance training modules
- Shadow team members on key workflows
- Attend team standups and sprint planning sessions
- Read key project documentation and architecture guides

### Day 3 Check-in
- One-on-one with your manager to address questions
- Review initial goals and adjust as needed
- Ensure all access and tools are working properly

### Day 5 Check-in
- Feedback session with your onboarding buddy
- Review first week experience
- Identify any blockers or additional support needed

## Week 2 — Active Contribution

### Activities
- Begin contributing to actual projects with guidance
- Attend cross-functional meetings relevant to your role
- Complete any remaining training modules
- Participate in your first sprint planning

### Day 10 Check-in
- Formal check-in with HR and manager
- Review progress against 30-60-90 day goals
- Provide onboarding experience feedback
- Confirm benefits enrollment and any remaining paperwork

## 30-60-90 Day Plan

### 30 Days — Learning
- Understand team processes and tools
- Complete all mandatory training
- Build relationships across the team
- Ask questions and seek feedback

### 60 Days — Contributing
- Take ownership of small projects or tasks
- Begin making independent decisions within scope
- Identify areas for professional development
- Provide constructive feedback to the team

### 90 Days — Independent
- Fully integrated into team workflows
- Operating independently in core responsibilities
- Identified and begun addressing development goals
- Ready for formal performance review

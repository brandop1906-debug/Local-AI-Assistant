# Project Kickoff Checklist

**Project Name:** _______________
**Project Manager:** _______________
**Kickoff Date:** _______________

## Pre-Kickoff

- [ ] Project charter drafted and approved
- [ ] Stakeholder list finalized
- [ ] Core team members identified
- [ ] Kickoff meeting scheduled (60-90 min)
- [ ] Project space created (GitHub repo, wiki, etc.)
- [ ] Initial timeline and milestones defined
- [ ] Budget approved
- [ ] Risk assessment completed

## Kickoff Meeting Agenda

### Part 1: Context (15 min)
- Business problem and opportunity
- Project goals and success criteria
- Scope overview (what's in and out of scope)
- Key stakeholders and their roles

### Part 2: Team & Process (15 min)
- Team structure and roles
- Communication channels and cadence
- Tooling and workflows
- Decision-making process

### Part 3: Planning (20 min)
- Timeline and major milestones
- Resource allocation
- Dependencies and constraints
- Initial risk register

### Part 4: Q&A (10 min)
- Open discussion
- Clarification of expectations

### Part 5: Next Steps (5 min)
- Immediate action items
- Next meeting scheduled
- Contact for questions

## Post-Kickoff

- [ ] Meeting notes distributed within 24 hours
- [ ] All team members have tool access
- [ ] First sprint/work cycle begins
- [ ] Stakeholder update sent
- [ ] Project tracking initialized
- [ ] Risk register updated with new risks
